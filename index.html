About Me

I am a recent graduate from UC Berkeley with a BS in Molecular Environmental Biology. During undergrad, I spent my days studying the broad range topics encompassing Earth System and Environmental Sciences ranging from cellular biology to landscape-level geomorphology. When I wasn't in the books, I was conducting climate change mitigation research in the field of soil science as a member of the Silver Lab group. Outside of academics, I love playing volleyball, running, going to the beach, and playing board games. Recently, I began a Full Stack Web Develop program offered through UC Berkeley Extension's Coding Bootcamp. My research experiences in undergrad exposed me to coding for data analytics in Matlab, Python, and R, and I found myself really enjoying the process.
